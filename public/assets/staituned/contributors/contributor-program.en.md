## üîÑ Contribution Paths

Choose the level of support you need. In any case, the final content will be optimized by our team to ensure SEO, GEO (Generative Engine Optimization), and maximum authority.

### 1. Autonomy Path ‚Äî "You write, we optimize"
Ideal if you already have a draft ready or prefer to write offline.
- **Workflow:** Define brief ‚Üí Accept agreement ‚Üí Upload draft.
- **Support:** Light editorial review, SEO optimization, graphic asset creation.
- **Estimated Time:** 1-3 hours (besides writing).

### 2. Guided Path ‚Äî "Idea to outline in 20 minutes"
Our most popular path. Perfect if you have the technical expertise but fear the "blank page" or want a solid structure to follow.
- **Workflow:** Define brief ‚Üí **Adaptive Interview (AI)** ‚Üí Source Analysis ‚Üí Outline Generation.
- **The Adaptive Interview:** You'll answer 5 dynamic questions generated by our "AI Senior Editor". The system digs into your insights to extract themes, technical mechanisms, and evidence.
- **What You Get:** A structured Markdown outline with H2/H3 headings, section-by-section prompts, and authoritative source suggestions (via Perplexity).
- **Estimated Time:** 20-30 minutes for the interview.

### 3. Interview Path ‚Äî "Tell us your vision"
Reserved for Experts, C-Levels, and Industry Leaders (by selection). Turn your expertise into thought leadership without writing a single line.
- **Workflow:** Pitch ‚Üí Deep-dive interview ‚Üí **Editorial drafting by stAItuned**.
- **What You Get:** A professional article ready for your signature, based exclusively on your experience and data collected during the interview.
- **Estimated Time:** 30-45 minutes of conversation.

## ‚ùì FAQ

### Do I need to be a professional copywriter?
No. We seek technical competence and strategic vision. We or our guided system handle the form and structure.

### Can I use AI to write?
Yes, as a writing aid. However, you remain solely responsible for accuracy, originality, and the absence of hallucinations or plagiarism.

### How are interview questions chosen?
Our engine analyzes your initial brief and identifies gaps. If the thesis is vague or examples are missing, the AI generates targeted questions to fill exactly those points.

### Who sees my interview answers?
Answers are processed by our models (Gemini/Perplexity) and are accessible to the stAItuned editorial team for review and support.
